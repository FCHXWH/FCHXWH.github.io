Biography   [back top]

I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Weikang Qian. I work on electronic design automation (EDA). Specifically, I am interested in logic synthesis algorithms and novel computing paradigms such as approximate computing. Earlier, I received the B.S. degree from Nanjing University of Science and Technology in 2018.

  • 09/2018 -- Now: Ph.D. student majoring in Electronic Science and Technology, ECTL Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China
  • 09/2014 -- 06/2018: B.S. in Communication Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China
