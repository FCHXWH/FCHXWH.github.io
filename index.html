Biography    [back top]

    I am currently a fifth-year PhD student at the Emerging Computing Technology Laboratory (ECTL) of SJTU. I am fortunate to work with Prof. Weikang Qian. I will receive my Ph.D. degree in June from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I study electronic design automation (EDA). My research interests lie in logic synthesis and analysis for emerging computing paradigms, such as approximate computing, stochastic computing.
